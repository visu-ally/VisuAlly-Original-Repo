Sweet Cleans Swift Wash Wipes To Go
This is the standard version of our product which cleans your glasses overnight by placing our special cloth on your glasses.

Key Details
  • Takes 7-8 hours to clean lenses
  • No need to use special liquid to recharge the cloth
  • Keep cloth in the container provided to help reduce dust accumulation
  • Great for long scheduled periods where glasses are not needed
    Ex: Overnight when you go to bed
 This version of our product is a faster alternative which cleans your glasses quickly, but it needs to be soaked in our special cleaning liquid after each use.

Key Details
  • Takes about 5 minutes to clean lenses
  • Must soak the cloth in our special liquid for about 3-4 hours before reusing it
  • Keep cloth in the container provided to help reduce dust accumulation
  • Great for quick cleaning when the time is available.
    Ex: While taking a shower
 As the name implies, this variation is good and easy to use on the go, but at the cost of the wipe being a one time use item.

Key Details
  • Takes about 1 minute to clean lenses
  • Cloth is one time use and biodigradable for easy disposal
  • Comes in packet sizes of 10, 25, 50 and 100 cloths which are easy to pack and bring anywhere
  • Great for emergency and uncalled for situations on the go.
    Ex: Glasses fall into some mud outside
